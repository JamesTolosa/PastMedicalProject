First aid for someone who may have meningitis









                            1.If someone has flu-like symptoms, a headache and a high
                               temperature, they may have meningitis. They may also complain of a
                               stiff neck and be sensitive to light. At a later stage a rash may form,
                               and the rash won’t disappear when a glass is pressed on it.

                                     Other symptoms of meningitis include cold hands and feet, joint pain,

                                     drowsiness and vomiting. A person with meningitis can have one or

                                     more symptoms, and some symptoms occur later on as the infection

                                     develops.






                            2.Call 911 if you see these symptoms.


                                     Do not wait for all the symptoms to appear. A person with meningitis

                                     can deteriorate very quickly. It is potentially very serious and needs

                                     immediate attention.





                            3. Reassure them until help arrives.

                                     If they have a fever, you can give the person cold drinks to cool them.
