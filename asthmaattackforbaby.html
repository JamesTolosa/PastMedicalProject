asthma attack for baby









                            1.Help the child sit in a comfortable

                               position and take their medication.


                                     When a child has an asthma attack, their

                                     airways narrow, making it difficult for them

                                     to breathe. An inhaler relaxes the muscles,

                                     allowing the airways to expand and ease

                                     their breathing.






                            2. Reassure the person. If the attack

                            becomes severe, or they don't have their

                            medication, call 911 as soon as possible.


                                     A mild attack should ease within a few

                                     minutes. Call 911 if the attack lasts longer

                                     than that, they don’t have their medication

                                     or the inhaler has no effect. Do not leave

                                     them, in case the attack becomes severe

                                     quickly. If you can’t call 999, get someone

                                     else to do it.
