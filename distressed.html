Learn first aid for someone who is distressed







                            1. Show you are listening. Calmly ask them

                            how you can help.


                                     Try to establish trust by listening to them respectfully.






                            2. Be considerate of what is going on around

                            them and what they need.


                                     Remember that they may have difficulty letting

                                     you know what they need. For example, they

                                     may not speak English very well or be able to

                                     hear you
